============================================================
              WHATSAPP AUTO-SENDER PRO
============================================================
Repository: https://github.com/Sarjin744/whatsapp-auto-sender-pro

This guide explains how to download, install, and use this
advanced WhatsApp Web Chrome Extension.

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: HOW TO DOWNLOAD
------------------------------------------------------------
If you are downloading this from GitHub:
1. Go to: https://github.com/Sarjin744/whatsapp-auto-sender-pro
2. Click the green "Code" button near the top right.
3. Select "Download ZIP".
4. Once downloaded, extract (unzip) the file to a folder on 
   your computer (e.g., your Desktop or Documents folder).

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: HOW TO INSTALL IN CHROME
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open Google Chrome.
2. In the address bar, type "chrome://extensions/" and press Enter.
3. In the top-right corner, turn ON the "Developer mode" switch.
4. In the top-left corner, click the "Load unpacked" button.
5. Select the folder where you extracted the extension 
   (the folder containing "manifest.json").
6. Click "Select Folder".
7. The extension "WhatsApp Auto-Sender Pro" will now appear 
   in your list!

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: HOW TO USE
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open WhatsApp Web (https://web.whatsapp.com) and log in.
2. Look for the floating Green Paper Plane button in the 
   top-right corner of the page. Click it to open the control panel.
3. Choose your mode:
   - SINGLE CHAT: Type a message and set how many times to repeat.
     Open the target chat and click "Start".
   - BULK SEND: Paste a list of phone numbers (one per line, e.g., 
     "919876543210, John"). Write a message using "{name}" for 
     personalization, and click "Start".
4. Monitor progress and active logs in the panel console.

------------------------------------------------------------
💡 PRO-TIPS FOR SAFETY (AVOID BANS)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Use Spintax (e.g., "{Hello|Hi|Hey} {friend|buddy}!") to generate
  random variations of your message so they don't look identical.
- Set a minimum delay of at least 0.5s to 1.5s between messages.
- Enable Cooldown Breaks (e.g., pause 30 seconds after every 15 
  messages) under the anti-ban settings panel.
